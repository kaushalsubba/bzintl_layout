James Du

CEO at BZ.IN.TL

FOLLOW

James is the Co-founder and CEO at Business Intelligence Pte Ltd, which operates BZ•IN•TL.

Prior to founding BZ•IN•TL, James served as the CEO of Asia Briefing Ltd., a wholly-owned subsidiary of Dezan Shira & Associates. He led all Business Development, Client Research, Creative Design and Web Development projects for multinational clients entering and expanding into Asia. At the parent firm Dezan Shira & Associates, he oversaw all marketing activities, global business publication, content marketing, technical knowledge sharing and web infrastructure development.

In the past, James has worked in management consulting and business development roles in the United States and in China. With an BFA from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor and an MBA from the University of Oxford, Pembroke College, he's highly passionate on the subject of how internet technology will help Asia interact with the rest of the world.
